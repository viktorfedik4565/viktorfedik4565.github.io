Translated sentences containing 'correr'

Si sigues así te correrán. If you keep going like that, they will fire you. Me gusta mucho correr. I like running very much. Nosotras tenemos que correr. We have to run. A nosotros nos gusta correr. We like to run. Vosotros no corréis rápido. You guys don t run fast. Corréis el riesgo de equivocaros. You all run the risk of making a mistake. Hay que correr. You should run. Vosotros ibais a correr en la mañana. You all were going to run in the morning. Yo corro en el parque. I am running in the park. Yo corría en el parque. I was running in the park.
