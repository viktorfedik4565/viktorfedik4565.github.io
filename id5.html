Correr Conjugation - Spanish Verb Conjugation - Conjugate Correr in Spanish

Correr is a Spanish regular er verb meaning to run . Correr appears on the 100 Most Used Spanish Verbs Poster as the 4th most used regular er verb.

For the preterite tense conjugation, go to Correr Preterite Tense Conjugation .

Correr Conjugation: Present Tense

yo corro tú corres él/ella corre nosotros/as corremos vosotros/as corréis ellos/ellas corren

Correr Participio

The participio of Correr is corrido. The present perfect tense is formed by combining the auxiliary verb haber with the participio.

Correr Gerundio

The gerundio of Correr is corriendo. The present continuous tense is formed by combining the auxiliary verb Estar with the gerundio.

Regular vs. Irregular Verbs

A verb is called a regular verb when its conjugation follows a typical pattern. A verb which does not follow these patterns exactly is called an irregular verb . In Spanish, the 3 regular patterns are for verbs ending in ar, er, and ir.
